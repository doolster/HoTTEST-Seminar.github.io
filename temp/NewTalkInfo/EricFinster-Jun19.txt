Title: Weak Structures from Strict Ones
YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wz3h59rnhOU
Slides: Finster-2020-Jun-19-HoTTEST.pdf
Abstract: I will describe a technique for defining various weak higher structures by extending type theory with a universe of strict polynomial monads.  Furthermore, I will describe implementation of the proposed theory in Agda using rewrite rules.
